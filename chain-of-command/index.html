Chain of Command

Introduction

The Chain of Command of the Los Reyes Guard (LRG) is an extensive and detailed organisation structure, made to guide operators and external users alike on who to contact and for what.

Repeated failure to follow the Chain of Command may result in infractions.

Command Authority

The Command Authority serves as the leaders of the Los Reyes Guard (LRG), being the most important officers. They are not to be contacted under any circumstance, excluding events such as server security or major contracts.


Command Authority Ranks:

  • Chief Executive Commander (CEC)
  • Deputy Chief Executive Commander (DCEC)
  • Director of Executive Command (DEC)
  • Deputy Director of Executive Command (DDEC)

Executive Command

The Executive Command consists of the Directors and Chiefs of the LRG. They are in charge of different aspects of the company, lead by the Executive Commanders and managed by the Chief Operations Officer.


Executive Command Ranks:

  • Chief Operations Officer (COO)
  • Director of Tactical Services (DTS)
  • Chief Intelligence and Threat Assessment Officer (CITAO)
  • Director of Corporate Security (DOCS)

High Commissioned Officers

The High Commissioned Officers are field officers and task force commanders. They are the most senior non-executive personnel.


High Commissioned Officers Ranks:

  • Field Marshall (FM)
  • Task Force Commander (TFC)
  • Senior Combat Strategist (SCS)

Commissioned Officers

Commissioned Officers are the Captains, Lieutenants and recon officers of the LRG. They are in charge of units of various sizes and are assigned long-term duties.


Commissioned Officers Ranks:

  • Operations Captain (OC)
  • Operations Lieutenant (OLT)
  • Recon Commander (RECOM)

Mission Specialists

Mission Specialists, similar to a military NCO, are operators who have shown extensive knowledge within their fields, earning them these ranks.
Those who perform exceptionally within leadership are eventually promoted to Unit Sergeant, either leading a small unit of operators or assisting a Commissioned Officer of leading a larger unit.


Mission Specialists Ranks:

  • Unit Sergeant (USGT)
  • Specialist of Security (SOSEC)
  • Tactical Intelligence Officer (TINTO)

Field Operators

Field Operators are the backbone of the LRG. They make up for the largest percentage of LRG personnel and serve as the mission operatives or security guards.


Field Operators Ranks:

  • Operator First Class (OFC)
  • Operator (OPT)
  • Junior Operator (JUOP)

Internal Affairs

The Internal Affairs works with any and all internal inquires. They serve as both support agents and leads in internal investigations. Flevie has served as the Internal Affairs Director since the 8th of December, 2025.


Internal Affairs Ranks:

  • Internal Affairs Director (IAD)
  • Internal Affairs Supervisor (IAS)
  • Senior Internal Affairs Agent (SIAA)
  • Internal Affairs Agent (IAA)
