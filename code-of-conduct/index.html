Los Reyes Guard™

Operator Code of Conduct

Introduction

As a Private Military Company, the Los Reyes Guard (LRG) maintains strict operational, ethical, and professional standards. All Contractors and Operators are required to uphold these principles during missions, training, and all community interactions associated with LRG.

Failure to meet these expectations may result in suspension, contract termination, or permanent removal from LRG.

I. Mandatory Compliance — Roblox Operational Zone

All LRG personnel operating within Roblox environments must comply with the Roblox Terms of Use and Community Standards.



Contractor Obligations:
  • Conduct yourself with discipline; harassment, discrimination, and hostile language are prohibited.

  • Do not utilize exploits, automation software, or unauthorized system modifications.

  • Maintain secure operational accounts - no account sharing, impersonation, or evasion of moderation actions.


Violations of Roblox regulations may lead to immediate termination of your LRG contract.

II. Mandatory Compliance — Discord Communications Network

LRG’s communications infrastructure operates under Discord’s Terms of Service and Safety Guidelines.



Contractor Obligations:
  • Communications must remain respectful, controlled, and free of threats or abusive behavior.
  • Distribution of NSFW, malicious, illegal, or harmful content is strictly prohibited.
  • All personnel must use a verified, personal Discord account and meet age requirements.


Breaches of Discord ToS will result in removal from LRG communication channels and further internal action.

III. LRG Internal Regulations

Chain of Command Discipline
LRG utilizes a strict hierarchical structure. Contractors must follow instructions from Supervisors, Officers, and Command Staff. Disputes or concerns must be reported through authorized channels.

Professional & Tactical Conduct
Contractors are expected to behave with operational discipline, situational awareness, and maturity. Any conduct on-duty or off-duty that undermines LRG’s professionalism or reputation is unacceptable.

Operational Channel Usage
All communication channels serve designated purposes. Off-topic chatter, clutter, or interference in operational discussions is prohibited.

External Recruitment & Promotion
Promotion of external units, groups, or organizations without Command authorization is not permitted.

IV. Enforcement & Disciplinary Measures

LRG Command reserves the right to adjust disciplinary actions based on severity, operational impact, and intent.

Distribution of NSFW content results in an immediate and irreversible contract termination from LRG.

For operational clarification or administrative support, contact an LRG Staff Member or open a ticket via our Discord support channel
